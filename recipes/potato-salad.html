Potato Salad

A Potato Salad Recipe

Everyone's favorite Salad, with potatoes in it!

Ingredients

  • Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • White Vinegar
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Celery
  • Green Onion
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes
  2. Peel&Slice them
  3. Sprinkle vinegar and make the dressing

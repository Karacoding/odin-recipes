A Chocolate Pudding Recipe

I hope everyone reading this can enjoy the pudding, even my lactose intolerant friends and family

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Egg Yolks
  • 2 Tbsp. Cornstarch
  • 2 cups Whole Milk, Divided
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 1/4 tsp. Salt
  • 2 tsp. Chocolate or Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Medium bowl, whisk egg for 60s, whisk in cornstarch and milk and set aside
  2. Scald Sugar,Cocoa,Salt and rest of the milk in saucepan
  3. Keep whiskin, temper it so eggs dont get scrambled
  4. Remove pudding from heat and add inthe extract, pour it into a bowl and press plastic on the surface so no skin forms
